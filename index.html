Who We Are

One Tribe. One Mission.

TEENS SUMMER CAMP 2026, ABUJA EDITION

The Jesus Tribe Abuja Teens’ Summer Camp continues to be a watershed event for our teenagers. It provides teenagers with an opportunity to disconnect from distractions, encounter God, build meaningful friendships, and have personal reflections.It is also our most effective teen outreach platform, with teens always eager to bring their friends, both churched and unchurched.

Over its past 3 editions, we have seen lives transformed through salvation, rededication, and the infilling of the Holy Spirit. Teenagers have stepped into leadership, formed wholesome relationships, and received valuable guidance for life and purpose.

2026

19–23 August · Abuja

Theme: Different by Design

The 2026 edition themed: “Different by Design” (1 Peter 2:9) is aimed at deepening the teenagers' conviction of their identity in Christ and inspire them to boldly live out their faith.

As we cultivate an atmosphere where teenagers can encounter God deeply, build meaningful relationships, and collaborate with like-minded believers, we trust that this year’s camp will further equip and empower them to lead change and impact their generation for Christ.

The Curriculum

What Teens Will Learn

The camp curriculum is designed to nurture the whole person — spiritually, socially, and practically. Every session is intentional, age-appropriate, and rooted in Scripture.

Biblical Teaching

Teens will be taught foundational Biblical principles that deal with their time as teens and how to implement the principles in their daily lives.

Spiritual Growth

Teens will receive training with practical sessions on how to deepen their faith through prayer, worship and quiet reflection.

Life Skills

Teens will be part of activities that allow for expression of known skills as well as develop new skills such as creative arts, public speaking, positive content creation, entrepreneurship, etc.

Fellowship

Teens will find a safe space to interact with peers and teachers, express themselves, interact, learn and build life-lasting relationships.

Leadership Development

Teens will be taught communication, empathy, decision making, teamwork and collaboration skills that allow them to be effective leaders amongst their peers and in their communities.
