📋 Essentials

Dear Parents and Teenagers,
Kindly find below the full list of items your teenager will need for camp, organized by category so nothing gets left behind.

🛏️ Bedding & Toiletries

  • Bed sheets – compulsory
  • Blanket or cover cloth
  • Bath towel and face towel
  • Laundry bag and bucket
  • Umbrella / raincoat
  • Mosquito net
  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Bathing soap and washing soap
  • Tissue paper (2 packs)
  • Body cream / lotion
  • Sanitary pads
  • Hair brush / comb
  • Deodorant / roll-on
  • Small padlock – for box/locker

👕 Clothing

General Clothing

  • Changes of clothes (3–4 sets)
  • Slippers – for shower
  • Sportswear and sport shoes
  • Sandals / comfortable walking shoes
  • Cardigan or jacket – for cold nights
  • Sets of underwear
  • Socks
  • Sleepwear – pyjama trousers or dresses
  • Church/service outfit
  • Cultural Attire – for variety night
  • Handkerchief

👔 Dress Code

  • Knee-length shorts
  • No cut-off sleeve tops
  • No slashed jeans
  • No muscle tops
  • No tops with inappropriate writing
  • Hair should be kept neat
  • Minimum length of shorts is below the knee
  • No tight-fitting tops or half tops
  • No transparent or spaghetti-strap tops
  • No slashed jeans
  • No tops with inappropriate writing
  • Knee-length skirts, dresses, and shorts appropriate for evening services

🎒 Personal Effects

These items apply to every camper, regardless of gender.

  • Bible – the only book aside from writing materials permitted
  • Notebook and pen
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Waist pouch or small backpack
  • Name tags or labels on all properties

💊 Medicals

• Campers with asthma MUST bring at least one personal inhaler and keep it within comfortable reach.

• Campers on medication for any condition should bring the medication to camp and report it to their Tribe head.

• Please list any allergies (food, drug, or otherwise) on the registration form ahead of camp.

🚫 Restricted Items

  • Electronic gadgets (phones, laptops, game consoles, iPads, smart watches, iPods)
  • Cash for purchasing food and beverages – meals and snacks are provided
  • Any books other than writing materials and a Bible
  • Precooked, bottled, or canned provisions

"Searching is a condition of entry, attendance, or continued attendance."

— Camp Board

Camp Board may conduct security searches of clothing, bags, and other items at its reasonable discretion.
Items deemed inappropriate will be confiscated and returned at the end of the program.
